I am a Computer Vision PhD student in the [VISTA](https://www.lix.polytechnique.fr/vista/) team (formerly [GeoViC](https://www.lix.polytechnique.fr/geovic/)) at [LIX](https://www.lix.polytechnique.fr/), [École Polytechnique](https://www.polytechnique.edu/en), under the supervision of [Vicky Kalogeiton](https://vicky.kalogeiton.info/) and [Marc Christie](http://people.irisa.fr/Marc.Christie/). My research explores the intersection of deep learning and cinematography across two primary axes: extracting cinematic features and generating multi-modal cinematic content.

Currently, I am a **Visiting PhD Researcher** at **University College London (UCL)** hosted by [Iasonas Kokkinos](https://jkokkinos.github.io/), where my work focuses on RL for human–human interactions.
